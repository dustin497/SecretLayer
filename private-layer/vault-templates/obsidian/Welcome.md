# Welcome to your Dark Side vault

This Obsidian vault lives inside your VeraCrypt volume. PrivateLayer indexes and edits notes here.

## Folders to create

- `plans/` — weekly and quarterly plans
- `clients/` — client notes and deliverables
- `profiles/` — business profile exports (YAML source in vault root)
- `tasks/` — action items and checklists

## Link your assistant

Start the agent: `python -m agent serve`  
Start Ollama with your model. Open PrivateLayer desktop → Assistant.

Your model. Your vault. Your rules.
